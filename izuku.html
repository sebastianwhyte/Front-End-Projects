Izuku Midoriya

Background

Izuku Midoriya is the main character of the popular anime and manga series, My Hero Academia.
Unlike most kids his age, 14 year old Izuku was born without a quirk. While growing up, he has looked up to All Might, a legendary hero. After meeting, he agrees to put timid Izuku through 10 months of training. Impressed by Izuku's dedication, All Might transfers the quirk, 'One for All' onto his new pupil.
Izuku must learn to control to his new powerful quirk to fight against villians and prove himself to his fellow UA peers. He soon will become the world's greatest hero.

Abilities

One For All - This quirk was transferred from All Might onto Izuku, who was quirkless at the time. One For All gives Izuku superhuman strength and speed. While using this quirk, Izuku can release gusts of wind, destroy objects and jump far into the air. As a way to prevent this powerful quirk from being overused, One For All will break bones of whatever body part was used. In order to prevent this, Izuku can only use 20% of its full power.

Black Whip - This is Izuku's second quirk. Like One For All, Izuku inherited this quirk. Black Whip allows Izuku to release dark energy from his arms.

Trivia


  1. "Deku" is a nickname given to Izuku by his rival/childhood friend, Katsuki. At first, it was intended to be an insult by Katsuki but later on would become a nickname that Izuku's friends would use.

  2. Izuku is a student of UA's Class 1-A.

  3. Izuku shares the same birthdate as Ichigo Kurosaki, whom is the protagionst of a popular shonen magna, Bleach.

  4. During his entrance exam, Izuku got 7th place and last place during the Quirk Apprehension Test.

  5. Despite scoring well during the Entrance Exams and Quirk Apprehension Test, Izuku ranked 4th in his class for grades.
