Background

This is my first sample webpage that focuses on my favorite anime, NARUTO. Originally published in Japan, the manga was written by the then 25 year-old Masashi Kishimoto. Naruto made its debut in the Weekly Shonen Jump 1999. The manga rapidly gained popularity and was adapted into two animated series, NARUTO and NARUTO: Shippuden. As of 2020, the NARUTO manga has sold over 250 million copies worldwide and the anime (both original and Shippuden) have 720 episodes altogether.

Summary

Naruto is a 12 year-old boy living in Konohagakure, also known as 'The Village Hidden in The Leaves' or "Hidden Leaf Village". Shortly after his birth, a giant nine-tailed demon fox raged havoc on Konoha. Following an intense battle, the Fourth Hokage sacrificed himself and sealed the demon fox within Naruto's body. Because of this, Naruto is ostracized from his fellow villagers. Regardless of the obstacles he's encountered during his life, Naruto is determined to fufill his dream: becoming Hokage.
